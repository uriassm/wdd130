Frequently Asked Questions

What is the new information system?

The new information system is a new updated website for you to access customer information and complete orders. The website keeps a familiar user interface while establishes a cleaner design and greater functionality. Most interactions with customers should bring you to using this website.

Where do I find training needed for my specific role?

New training will be assigned to you every year and you will receive an email notification. To access your training courses, visit the Learning Center. You may also enroll in courses you may not have been assigned but need to complete your work.

How do I report bugs that I experience?

You can report any bugs on the new website using the Feedback link at the bottom of each page.
