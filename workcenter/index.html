Welcome!

Welcome to the company website and hub for all things related to your role. Catch up on recent news and updates throughout the organization. Read articles, find inspirational stories, and feel connected to those across the company spectrum. We want you to always feel part of the company culture and know that you are the best part of what makes the organization work. Throughout the website you will also find training associated with your role including quarterly training. Finally, there will be a list of FAQs to answer common questions for reaching out to leadership and working with software.

HR System

The new HR system has now been launched and is ready to use for all team members. Find new exciting options like adding new gained skills to match jobs within the company, enter and correct timesheets online, and see personal information like your pay stub. This new system will also be used to enter your development goals for the year. Both you and your manager will have access to your goals online so you can work together to find success in your current role and move forward in your career path. If you use a time clock for entering your hours you will also find this tool here. The clock will continue to record the time spent at work even if you are not viewing the site. Throughout the following months we will continue to rollout new options and exciting features meant to create a hub for all work related items. All new announcements will be posted here. For any further questions, refer to your manager or review the FAQs section for common questions.
