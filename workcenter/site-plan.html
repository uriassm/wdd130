Overview

Purpose

Do you ever feel that important training information gets lost too soon when introducing new concepts to your team at work? Does outdated information sit too long on the desk of one of your team members? It's so easy in the normal work day for a printed memo to be shoved in a growing stack of papers. Passing out paper every time there is an update always comes with these risks. It would be more effective to have single source of current information that team members can always have access to. This is where an internal website comes in. A place where guides, updates, and important information can be maintained so everyone on your team can have the needed knowledge to work towards a unified goal.

Audience

The audience are those who work in a professional setting. They work in a team that constantly need to communicate and have reliable access to a computer or device that has internet connectivity. The site gives team members confidence that they are viewing reliable information. It will help managers feel capable of keeping their team together and on the same page. The site is easy to navigate to ensure all team members feel comfortable finding needed information.

Learning Center

Get training on the new Information System to log in orders and view customer information. Through these modules you will go through step-by-step simulations that will teach common everyday tasks to complete interactions with customers. All modules have both an audio and subtitles option for greater flexibility in taking these courses.

You will now be able to view the entire order schedule online and look up customer information through a search engine. Policies and procedures are enforced within the new Information System so you will have a guided experience each time you are creating a new order. Required information will need to be entered before continuing with the order.

Migrate beyond email and begin collaborating with Microsoft Teams. Future meetings and chat messaging should be used within this platform. This option allows for easy file sharing and communicating with team members. Further understanding with using apps for this software will be covered in the contained training. Any questions can also be forwarded to your manager to implement using this communication method in the best possible way for your department.
